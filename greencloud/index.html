Features

Why Greencloud?

Sustainable

Greencloud care about sustainability in every step of our production process, we use 100% renewable energy from local windmills, reduce consumption by carefully selecting hardware, and build office with renewable materials.

Data Privacy

Greencloud regards the Internet as a medium for accessible information, a platform to express opinions, that's why we play an active role in data privacy and internet freedom. We do as much as we can to protect your data and privacy.

Infrastructure owned by ourselves

Greencloud fully owns and controls hardware, infrastructure and uplinks. This is not only about security and privacy, but also means we can reduce costs and provide high-performance servers.
